# Notices

This repository contains an unofficial educational study aid. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Federal Aviation Administration.

## FAA Source Material

Learner-facing 7110 content is intended to cite FAA JO 7110.65 source locations so users can verify generated or curated study material against the current official order. Aircraft type-designator material is supporting reference material derived from FAA JO 7360.1K, not JO 7110.65 paragraph content.

This project must not represent generated explanations, questions, flashcards, or activities as operational guidance. Current FAA orders, facility directives, LOAs, training materials, and instructor or supervisor direction control.

## Generated Study Content

Questions, activities, explanations, and flashcards may be generated or curated. They require QA review for:

- Accurate source grounding.
- Correct operational framing.
- Appropriate difficulty and educational purpose.
- Avoidance of misleading rote-verbatim prompts where concept application is more appropriate.

## Aircraft Images

Aircraft image candidates are not learner-facing content until a human verifies aircraft identity, recognition usefulness, and reuse metadata. Public/static builds should not expose internal image-review tooling or unreviewed image candidate files.

When an approved aircraft image is used, keep attribution and license provenance available through the source panel rather than mixing it into the learner's aircraft-fact answer.
